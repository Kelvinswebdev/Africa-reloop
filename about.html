About African Reloop


We are African Reloop. The sustainable future company. We believe in the power of recycling to transform lives. By turning waste into valuable resources, we unlock economic opportunities, protect our environment, and build healthier communities across Africa. Waste management is more than just disposing of trash—it's about rethinking how we use and reuse resources. Every discarded item has the potential to be repurposed, reducing pollution and conserving natural resources for future generations.


However, as Africa’s population grows, so do the challenges. By 2050, the continent will be home to over 2.5 billion people, making sustainable waste management more critical than ever. We must act now to implement effective solutions that balance economic growth with environmental responsibility.

At African Reloop, we are committed to innovation in recycling, waste reduction, and circular economy practices. Our work is about creating a greener, cleaner Africa by educating communities, partnering with businesses, and pushing the boundaries of sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable recycling practices that create economic opportunities, protect the environment, and improve the well-being of African communities.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in Africa’s recycling industry, inspiring communities to embrace a waste-free, environmentally responsible future.

Our Core Values

Innovation: We embrace creative solutions to recycling challenges.

Sustainability: We prioritize long-term environmental and economic impact.

Community: We work together to build healthier, empowered communities.

Responsibility: We are committed to ethical and sustainable recycling practices.
